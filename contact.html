Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), UPI payments (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), net banking, and PayPal. All transactions are secured with SSL encryption.

How long does it take to set up my server?

Most servers are set up instantly after payment (within 1-2 minutes). In rare cases during high demand, it might take up to 15 minutes. You'll receive an email with your server details as soon as it's ready.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on all our hosting plans. If you're not satisfied with our service within the first 7 days, you can request a full refund with no questions asked.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time through your client area. The changes will take effect immediately, and you'll only pay the prorated difference for the remaining billing period.

What kind of support do you offer?

We provide 24/7 support through live chat, email, and Discord. Our average response time is under 15 minutes. We also have an extensive knowledge base with tutorials and guides for self-help.
