About HexGen

Learn about our mission, vision, and the team behind India's fastest gaming hosting platform

Our Story

Founded in 2025, HexGen was born out of frustration with the lack of high-quality, low-latency gaming hosting options for Indian gamers. Our founders, Zenova, Tino and VisionViews themselves, recognized the need for a hosting provider that could deliver exceptional performance with servers located right here in India.

What started as a small operation with just a few servers has now grown into one of India's leading gaming hosting providers, serving thousands of happy customers across the country.

Our Mission

At HexGen, our mission is simple: to provide Indian gamers with the best possible hosting experience. We believe that every gamer deserves access to high-performance servers with minimal latency, regardless of their budget.

We're committed to continuous innovation, exceptional customer support, and maintaining the highest standards of server performance and reliability.
